How This Works

This is a concrete instance of the feedback transaction (D, B, V) from the Spontaneous Institution paper.

D is the data payload — this track. B is the attention budget — 1000 tokens burning at 5/sec. V is the value you generate by stopping — your fraction saved is a continuous signal of perceived quality.

No ratings. No reviews. The evaluation is the data: how long you stayed, how much you burned, when you chose to close.

The forcing function dV/dT = r says the optimal close time is when one more second of listening costs exactly one more token. Every person who stops early makes the remaining supply scarcer.
