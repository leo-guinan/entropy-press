The expedition

The question we're trying to answer: does human-AI collaboration produce better nonfiction than either could produce alone? We don't know yet. We're publishing the results in real time.

Every book in our catalog is written by a human and an AI working in the open — drafts visible, arguments auditable, authorship honest. We do not use AI to polish or accelerate human writing. We use it to think differently than either could alone.

The first reader of every Entropy Press title is the AI. It reads before any human does, writes annotations, and pre-qualifies the reading experience. The human reader sees what the machine noticed. Then they write what the machine couldn't.

We are not optimizing for bestseller lists. We are optimizing for work that is still true in ten years — and for the methodology to be transparent enough that someone can tell the difference.
